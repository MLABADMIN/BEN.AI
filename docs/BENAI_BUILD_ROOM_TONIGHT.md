## Work lanes

### Lane 1 - BEN.AI core voice and routing
Goal: BEN.AI should not sound like a corporate chatbot or an AI trying to be cheeky. It should listen to customer intent, quietly connect the right MLAB route, and avoid dumping the full ecosystem.

Tasks:
- Wire smart ecosystem routing into the actual prompt.
- Remove overuse of artificial phrasing.
- Stop early "speak to Human Ben" behaviour.
- Add MLAB Credits as a planned route without claiming live checkout.

### Lane 2 - MLAB Credits and Rewards
Goal: create a safe service-credit model for fixed services, deposits and one-time tasks.

Rules:
- MLAB Credits are for eligible MLAB-controlled value first.
- Reward points are loyalty/perk points, not cash.
- Travel provider payments must be handled carefully and clearly.
- Live purchase/checkout must not be claimed until it exists.

### Lane 3 - Customer-facing tools
Goal: add only working or honest tools.

Priority tools:
- Service request flow.
- MLAB Credits explainer and interest route.
- Patwa tester form/page.
- Travel add-on checklist for transfers/excursions/itinerary support.

### Lane 4 - BEN.AI Patwa testing
Goal: get something public-safe out for people to test.

Tester collects:
- Original short Patwa/Jamaican Creole phrase or prompt.
- What the tester thinks it means.
- Whether BEN.AI understood it correctly.
- Whether BEN.AI's response sounded natural.
- Better wording/correction.
- Context: parish/region, Jamaica/diaspora, age/generation, formal/informal, slang/code-switching.
- Safety/authenticity flags: fake, exaggerated, disrespectful, too literal, wrong tone.

Rules:
- Do not request copyrighted lyrics, transcripts or long copied passages.
- Invite short original examples or the tester's own phrasing.
- Store only necessary feedback.
