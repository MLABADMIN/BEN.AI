# BEN.AI Patwa Tester - Basic Version

## Goal
Create a simple tester for BEN.AI's Jamaican Patwa / Jamaican Creole understanding.

The tester should help check:
- meaning
- translation
- naturalness
- tone
- better wording
- context

## Public tester note
Please share only short original examples or your own wording. Do not paste songs, long transcripts, private messages or anything you do not have permission to share.

## Form fields

1. Short phrase or prompt
2. What you think it means in English
3. What you wanted BEN.AI to do
4. Did BEN.AI understand correctly?
5. Did BEN.AI sound natural?
6. Better wording or correction
7. Context notes such as region, generation, Jamaica or diaspora, formal or casual, slang or code-switching
8. Authenticity flags such as fake, exaggerated, too literal, wrong tone, disrespectful or needs more context
9. Anything else BEN.AI should know

## Review labels
- meaning
- translation
- grammar
- tone
- naturalness
- context
- safety
- code-switching

## Confirmation message
Thank you for helping BEN.AI improve Jamaican Patwa / Jamaican Creole understanding with care.
